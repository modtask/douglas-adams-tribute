Douglas Adams

Author of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

Here is a time line of Douglas Adams' life.

  • 1952 - Born in Cambridge, England
  • 1979 - Published The Hitchhiker's Guide To The Galaxy, the first of five books in the Hitchhiker series.
  • 1980 - Published The Restaurant at the End of the Universe, the second of five books in the Hitchhiker series.
  • 1982 - Published Life, the Universe, and Everything, the third of five books in the Hitchhiker series.
  • 1984 - Published So Long, and Thanks for All the Fish, the fourth of five books in the Hitchhiker series.
  • 1992 - Published Mostly Harmless, the fifth of five books in the Hitchhiker series.
  • 2001 - dies at the age of 49.
